About Me

I'm mostly from Madison, Wisconsin. I went to college in Cleveland and received my BS and MS in applied math at Case Western Reserve University. No, it is not a military school, it is named after the Western Reserve, which is territory that includes most of Northeastern Ohio. The "Case" part comes from the Case Institute of Technology (CIT) which mmerged with Western Reserve University in the 70s. Both universities were top-tier research institutions with CIT second only to Cal Tech, until the Ohio river burst into flames and brain drain plagued the area for the next several decades.

I used to play a lot of shows, but have mostly retired from music as a hobby. The main part of my repertoire includes guitar (since 2002) and ukulele (since 2015).
